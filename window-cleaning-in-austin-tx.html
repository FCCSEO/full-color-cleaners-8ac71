How often should I have my gutters cleaned in Austin considering the local oak trees?
In Austin, where oak trees are prevalent, we recommend having your gutters cleaned at least twice a year - typically in late fall after most leaves have fallen, and in spring after oak pollen season. However, if your property has several oak or other trees nearby, you may need more frequent cleaning to prevent clogging and water damage.
